About NewsAnalyzer

NewsAnalyzer is an AI-powered tool designed to help you quickly understand the latest news trends. Simply enter your search query, and NewsAnalyzer will gather and analyze articles to deliver valuable insights.

Here's how it works:

  • News Collection: News articles are fetched from NewsAPI based on your search query.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Each article is evaluated to determine whether its tone is positive or negative.
  • Category Classification: Articles are automatically categorized into topics like business, technology, politics, and more.
  • Summarization: Long descriptions are summarized to give you only the most important details.
  • Trending Words: The most frequently mentioned words across articles are extracted to highlight key topics.
  • Charts: Sentiment distribution and trending words are visualized with clear, easy-to-understand charts.

Technologies:

NewsAnalyzer combines real-time data collection with machine learning models from Hugging Face, using Flask to seamlessly connect the data processing, analysis, and visualization into a single streamlined experience.
